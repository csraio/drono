Events & Festivities in the Pavilion



To honour the culture of the Gupta Empire, the pavilion will hold celebrations in correspondence with major events/milestones in the history of the empire. The pavilion will recognize official holidays in its designated country/state but it is not planned for the pavilion to hold an event or close or do something in that vein. However, the pavilion will recognize major Hindu holidays, since that was the main religion of the empire. Such holidays include, Diwali, Holi, Navratri, etc. Whether we will hold festivities of said major Hindu holidays is still not confirmed, but is very probable. Please stay informed as the official list of holidays recognized in the pavilion will be growing in the near future.
