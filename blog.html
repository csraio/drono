Friday 1/29/21


Today, we looked at the various UN Sustainable Development Goals and brainstormed possible solutions to these issues. Some of our ideas included masks that create water, an electric-powered bicycle, and a trash sensor to be used in cities. However, we were still unable to finalize our topic.


Saturday 1/30/21

Today was the day we finally decided on our project. We decided to advance in the idea of a trash-sensor system. This eventually evolved into an aeriel drone-collection system that picked up trash cubes that a ground-robot had collected and compacted. Today was also the day that we gained experience in TinkerCAD. We built an autonomous washing machine system that would come to you instead of you going to it to collect your. This silly project provided us with invaluable experience in TinkerCAD that helped us create our robot/drone designs for our actual project.


Sunday 1/31/21

Today, we polished up our website. With most of it finished on Saturday night, there wasn’t much left to do. We added some images, implemented some JavaScript, and reminisced about our hard work in creating this project. Although it was long and difficult, our team made something that we were truly proud of: a website created truly from scratch
