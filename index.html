Trouble In The City


Cities are majestic places in which trade thrives, opportunities expand, and millions of people live. But it is also a place where a lot of trash can accumulate. According to The World Bank, cities around the world generated about 2.01 billion tonnes of solid waste in 2016!


So what is wrong in the cities?

If this trash is not thoroughly managed and removed, then there can be some major implications. For example, a city with a high concentration of trash can facilitate the spread of disease, lower the mental health of residents, and create a city which, overall, does not look aesthetically pleasing.


Well, what should we do about it?

In an effort to tackle this problem, DRONO implements both aerial and ground-based machines that can monitor, manage, and clean up the garbage in the city. With an effective method to clean this trash up, the previously mentioned problems can be successfully avoided.
