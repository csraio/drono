Ground Robot:


This robot will be based on the ground of the city. It will utilize Computer Vision to make sense of its surroundings and identify garbage that can be collected. Then, with its robotic arms, it will collect the trash and store it into container. Once this container fill up, it will be compacted into a small cube that will be left behind for the drone to pick up.
